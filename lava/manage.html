Systems Monitoring

Systems monitoring is another formational aspect of IT systems management, and when correctly executed, plays an essential role in the efficiency and security of your digital assets and structure. However, adept systems monitoring is also extremely responsive: it ensures that any potential disruptions are immediately handled.
It involves— you guessed it— monitoring systems (typically through centralizing software) and collecting data on functions such as applications, server memory, bandwidth, CPU, routers, switches, and much more. Systems monitoring is the communication of devices and applications with the monitoring system (software) through protocols such as HTTP, WMI, OPC UA, SNMP, and MQTT. When actionable items crop up, our 24/7 help desk technicians are always ready to respond.
Systems monitoring not only helps businesses to understand where their resources are being used and how performance is impacted; it’s also essential to security functions. Systems monitoring helps to develop cohesion and efficiency within a technological framework on all levels of operations. Is your systems monitoring protocol effective? Or are important flags slipping between the cracks? Call AEG Tech today to ensure all of your bases are covered.
